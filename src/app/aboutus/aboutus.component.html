About Us

The project “ALAMPAT” is a consumer-to-consumer (C2C) e-commerce application that aims to develop an online marketplace for handmade goods, art merchandise, and art commission services (i.e., customizations). This will help local and independent artists, crafters, and crafts shops to sell and advertise their products and services in a wider market with easy and convenient purchasing transactions for consumers during the pandemic. Transactions for deliveries and Cash on Delivery (COD) payments will be facilitated by local courier services, and for cashless or electronic payments will be handled through the payment service provider.

 

The project idea caters to the local independent artists to promote their art and crafts in the online market in Cebu. The platform allows independent artists and crafters to set up an online shop/store to sell handmade products or services. This gives opportunity to the artists to reach a bigger audience and increase their revenue. The platform also provides convenience to consumers looking for unique items and customization services.
